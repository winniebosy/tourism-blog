Scenic Attractions

Mount Kenya is one of the most famous scenic tourist attraction in Kenya.
It is famous for its three peaks namely: Batian, Nelion and Point Lenana.
In addition, its scenic landscapes and vast wildlife preserves are a major global attraction.
Its diverse wildlife and panoramic geography draw large numbers of European and North American visitors.
Tourism is an important contributor to both Nanyuki's and the entire Kenyan economy.
